Methodology

Overall Sustainability Performance Marikina City fulfilled the SDG 11 indicators based on both resident responses and institutional practices. Key Findings by Indicator Urban Planning & Human Settlements: Former informal settlements were relocated to better housing with services. Civil society participation in planning was high. Transport: Safe and accessible transport systems exist, including affordable and PWD-friendly options, though access is not uniform in all areas. Disaster Preparedness & Management: High awareness and implementation of emergency policies at the barangay level; majority find barangay disaster strategies effective. Waste Management: Materials Recovery Facilities (MRFs) exist in most barangays; strict segregation and reuse practices are common. Safety & Inclusivity: Programs for senior citizens, women, and persons with disabilities were recognized by most residents. Resident Satisfaction: A significant majority expressed satisfaction with local government services supportive of sustainability.
