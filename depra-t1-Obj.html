Objective

•Assess the antibiotic resistance patterns of E. coli strains from the Marikina River.

•Identify potential sources of contamination, which could include wastewater from households, industries, and agricultural runoff.

•Evaluate the environmental impact of these resistant strains on the river ecosystem.

•Determine the public health risks posed by the spread of antibiotic-resistant bacteria in water sources, which could affect people who use the river water for domestic or recreational purposes.

•Provide recommendations for mitigating antibiotic resistance in the environment and reducing the public health risks associated with it.
