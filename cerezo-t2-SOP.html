Biopolymers like starch have inherent limitations in application due to poor mechanical strength and high water affinity. There is a need for sustainable bioplastics with improved functional properties to serve in real-world applications like packaging. Scope Focus was on cassava starch as a biopolymer base due to its low cost, abundant availability, renewable nature, and biodegradability.
