Objectives

•The main goal of the study was to:

•Determine the differences in airborne microplastic (AMP) abundance and distribution at open-air hawker stalls in suburban, urban, and industrial coastal areas in Peninsular Malaysia, and to:

•Characterize the physical (shape, color) and chemical (polymer type) properties of airborne microplastics collected in these environments.

•This addresses an important gap in understanding microplastics in outdoor atmospheric environments and food-related exposure settings.
