Methodology

Materials and Formulation Cassava starch was used with a cassava starch : plasticizer ratio of 1 : 0.05 optimized for mechanical performance. Silver (Ag) and zinc oxide (ZnO) nanoparticles were synthesized by green methods and added to bioplastic formulations at varying concentrations. Characterization Techniques Nanoparticle characterization: X-ray diffraction (XRD), Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM), Transmission Electron Microscopy (TEM). Mechanical testing: Tensile strength measurements. Biodegradation studies: Conducted in soil, air, and water environments for 31 days to evaluate degradation behavior.
