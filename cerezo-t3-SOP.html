Research Design Mixed quantitative and qualitative design with key informant interviews, semi-structured interviews, and a resident survey. Data Sources Responses from 272 residents (drawn from a population of 531,128 in 2018 via random sampling across Marikina’s barangays). Interviews with city planning officers, department directors, and disaster risk reduction managers. Framework Sustainability indicators were based on UN SDG 11: • Urban planning and settlements • Transport systems • Natural heritage • Disaster preparedness and management • Waste management • Safety and non-discriminatory practices. Data Collection Tools Surveys and questionnaires administered to adults (≥18 years). Secondary data from local and national records (e.g., NDRRMC, UNDP, UN-Habitat).
